<p><strong>The Tabletop Optical Coherence Tomography Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spectral-Domain OCT</li><li>Swept-Source OCT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tabletop Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) devices are primarily categorized into two types: Spectral-Domain OCT and Swept-Source OCT. Spectral-Domain OCT utilizes a broadband light source and a spectrometer to capture high-resolution images, making it ideal for retinal imaging and assessing ocular conditions. In contrast, Swept-Source OCT employs a tunable laser that sweeps across different wavelengths, providing deeper tissue penetration and faster image acquisition. Both technologies play crucial roles in ophthalmic diagnostics and other medical applications, enhancing visualization and analysis.</p></p>
