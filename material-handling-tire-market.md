<p><p>Material handling tires are essential for optimizing the performance of forklift trucks and other industrial vehicles. Pneumatic tires are filled with air, providing better shock absorption and traction on uneven surfaces, making them suitable for outdoor environments. In contrast, cushion tires are solid rubber, designed for smooth surfaces, offering durability and stability in indoor settings. Both types serve distinct operational needs, with pneumatic tires excelling in rough terrains and cushion tires ideal for warehouses, contributing to efficient material handling processes.</p></p>
