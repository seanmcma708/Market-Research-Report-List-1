<p><strong>The Microplate Photometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Filter Microplate Reader</li><li>Optical Grating Microplate Reader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microplate photometers market is segmented primarily into two types: optical filter microplate readers and optical grating microplate readers. Optical filter microplate readers employ specific filters to isolate wavelengths of light, making them suitable for fast, high-throughput applications and routine assays. In contrast, optical grating microplate readers utilize diffraction gratings to achieve a broader wavelength range and higher sensitivity for more complex analyses. Both types play crucial roles in biochemical and clinical research, enabling precise measurement of sample properties.</p></p>
