<p><p>Arc Fault Detection Devices (AFDD) are advanced electrical safety devices that detect arc faults, which can lead to electrical fires if left unchecked. These devices monitor electrical circuits for abnormal patterns of current flow that indicate arcing, which occurs when electrical currents jump across gaps in wiring or connections. By distinguishing between harmless transient events and potentially hazardous arcs, AFDDs help enhance safety in residential and commercial buildings.</p><p>The AFDD market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increased awareness of electrical fire hazards, stringent safety regulations, and the rising demand for advanced safety devices in electrical installations.
