<p><ul><li>Semi-open Impeller</li><li>Open Impeller</li><li>Enclosed Impeller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The horizontal mixed flow pump market features three primary impeller types: semi-open, open, and enclosed impellers. Semi-open impellers offer a balance of efficiency and solids handling, ideal for varying fluid types. Open impellers excel in handling slurries and larger solids due to their unrestricted design, providing easier maintenance. Enclosed impellers, with their enclosed vanes, provide high efficiency and reduced wear, making them suitable for clean fluids under higher pressure operations. Each type caters to specific operational requirements in various industrial applications.</p></p>
