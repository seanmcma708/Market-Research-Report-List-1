<p><p>Over-The-Air (OTA) updates for the Internet of Vehicles (IoV) encompass two primary market types: Firmware Over-The-Air (FOTA) and Software Over-The-Air (SOTA). FOTA focuses on updating the vehicle's firmware, enhancing core functionalities, performance, and security. In contrast, SOTA targets application software, enabling the addition of new features and improvements to user experiences. Together, these update mechanisms ensure vehicles remain up-to-date, safe, and equipped with the latest technology, enhancing overall efficiency and user satisfaction in the IoV ecosystem.</p></p>
