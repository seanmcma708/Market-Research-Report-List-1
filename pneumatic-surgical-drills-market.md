<p><p>The pneumatic surgical drills market is divided into two main types: wired and wireless drills. Wired type drills are powered through a direct connection to a pneumatic system, providing consistent performance and reliability in various surgical procedures. In contrast, wireless type drills operate independently, relying on portable air compressors, offering greater maneuverability and convenience for surgeons. Both types are designed to enhance precision and efficiency in bone and tissue procedures, crucial for successful surgical outcomes.</p></p>
