<p><p>The cryogenic solenoid valve market encompasses several types designed for handling extreme low temperatures. Plunger valves feature a simple design that uses a plunger to open or close the flow, offering reliable performance. Direct-acting plunger valves operate without a pilot and directly respond to electrical signals, ensuring rapid actuation. Pilot-operated valves use a small pilot valve to control a larger main valve, providing enhanced efficiency and control over high pressures, making them suitable for various cryogenic applications.</p></p>
