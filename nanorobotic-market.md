<p><p>The nanorobotic market encompasses three main types: nanomanipulators, bio-nanorobots, and magnetically guided robots. Bio-nanorobots operate within biological systems, delivering targeted therapies at the cellular level, revolutionizing medicine and drug delivery. Magnetically guided robots utilize magnetic fields for navigation and control at the nanoscale, facilitating applications in medical diagnostics and targeted treatment, enhancing efficiency and effectiveness in various industries.</p></p>
