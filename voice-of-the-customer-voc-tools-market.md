<p><p>Voice of the Customer (VoC) tools can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based VoC tools are hosted online, offering flexibility, scalability, and ease of access for users. They typically require minimal IT involvement and provide regular updates. In contrast, on-premise VoC tools are installed locally within an organization’s infrastructure. While they offer greater control over data security and customization, these solutions often demand more resources for maintenance and updates. Both types aim to gather and analyze customer feedback effectively.</p></p>
