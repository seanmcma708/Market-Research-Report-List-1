<p><strong>The Coffee Harvesters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vibration Picking Machine (Derricadeira)</li><li>Stripping Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Coffee Harvesters market includes technologies like vibration picking machines (derricaideras) and stripping machines, each serving specific harvesting needs. Vibration picking machines utilize vibrations to gently shake coffee berries off branches, allowing for efficient harvests while reducing damage to trees. Stripping machines, on the other hand, remove all berries from branches in a single action, making them suitable for areas with homogeneous ripening. Both types improve harvesting efficiency and labor management, catering to modern coffee production demands.</p></p>
