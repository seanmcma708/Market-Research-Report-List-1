<p><p>The conveyor belt market consists of three primary types: lightweight, mediumweight, and heavyweight belts. Lightweight conveyor belts are ideal for transporting small, lightweight materials and are commonly used in packaging and food industries. Mediumweight belts handle heavier loads and are often employed in manufacturing and warehousing applications. Heavyweight conveyor belts are designed for demanding environments, capable of moving bulky and abrasive materials in industries like mining, construction, and bulk handling, ensuring durability and efficiency in transport operations.</p></p>
