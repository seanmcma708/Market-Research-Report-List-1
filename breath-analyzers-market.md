<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Breath Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fuel Cell</li><li>Semiconductor</li><li>Infrared</li><li>Crystal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Breath analyzers are categorized into four main types based on their technology. Fuel cell breath analyzers utilize an electrochemical reaction to measure alcohol levels accurately and are known for their reliability. Semiconductor models employ resistive sensor technology, commonly found in compact, cost-effective devices, though they may be less precise. Infrared analyzers leverage absorption spectroscopy to detect various gases for medical diagnostics and environmental monitoring. Lastly, crystal-based analyzers use piezoelectric sensors to analyze breath composition, providing rapid and sensitive results.</p></p>
