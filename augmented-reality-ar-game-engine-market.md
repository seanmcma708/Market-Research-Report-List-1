<p><p>The Augmented Reality (AR) game engine market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based AR game engines leverage remote servers for processing and storage, enabling developers to access powerful resources and share their applications seamlessly across multiple devices. This flexibility fosters collaboration and rapid updates. On-premises AR game engines, on the other hand, are installed locally on a user's hardware, providing enhanced control over performance, security, and resource management, making them ideal for organizations with specific requirements.</p></p>
