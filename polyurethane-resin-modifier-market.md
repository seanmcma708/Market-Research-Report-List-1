<p><ul><li>Monofunctional Modifier</li><li>Multifunctional Modifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane resin modifier market is segmented into two main types: monofunctional modifiers and multifunctional modifiers. Monofunctional modifiers contain a single reactive group, enhancing specific properties such as flexibility or adhesion without significantly altering the overall structure. In contrast, multifunctional modifiers have multiple reactive groups, enabling more complex chemical interactions, improved mechanical properties, and enhanced thermal stability. Both types play crucial roles in tailoring polyurethane formulations for diverse applications across industries like automotive, construction, and footwear.</p></p>
