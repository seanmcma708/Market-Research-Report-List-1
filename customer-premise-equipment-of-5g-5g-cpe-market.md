<p><p>Customer Premise Equipment (CPE) for 5G includes devices that connect end-users to 5G networks. The market is divided into two primary types: Indoor CPE and Outdoor CPE. Indoor CPE typically consists of routers or gateways designed for residential use, providing high-speed internet connectivity within homes and offices. In contrast, Outdoor CPE is built for external environments, often featuring stronger antennas and weather-resistant designs to enhance connectivity over longer distances and support larger areas, such as rural or suburban deployments.</p></p>
