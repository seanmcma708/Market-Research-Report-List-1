<p><p>The security screening equipment market encompasses various technologies designed to detect threats. Walk-Through Metal Detectors (WTMD) and Hand-Held Metal Detectors (HHMD) identify metallic objects on individuals. X-Ray Systems inspect bags and cargo for concealed items. Explosives Trace Detection (ETD) and Explosives Detection Systems (EDS) analyze substances for explosive materials, while Explosives Trace Portals (ETP) facilitate non-intrusive screening. Liquid Container Screening (LCS) assesses liquid contents in containers, and Threat Image Projection (TIP) Systems enhance operator training through simulated threats.</p></p>
