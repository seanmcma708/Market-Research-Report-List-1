<p><strong>The Robotic Pool Cleaner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crawler Drive</li><li>Wheel Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The robotic pool cleaner market is primarily segmented into two types: crawler drive and wheel drive systems. Crawler drive cleaners utilize tracks to navigate pool surfaces, providing superior traction and stability on uneven or textured surfaces. They excel in climbing walls and cleaning corners effectively. In contrast, wheel drive systems use wheels for movement, offering faster cleaning cycles on flat surfaces. While generally easier to maneuver, they may struggle with steep angles but are often more affordable and simpler in design.</p></p>
