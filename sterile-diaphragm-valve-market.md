<p><p>The sterile diaphragm valve market is segmented into several types based on actuation methods. Manual diaphragm valves require physical operation for control, providing simplicity and reliability in low-automation settings. Pneumatic diaphragm valves use compressed air for actuation, offering rapid response and precision, ideal for dynamic processes. Electric diaphragm valves employ electric actuators, allowing for remote control and integration with automation systems. Other types may include hybrid systems or innovative designs that combine features of the primary categories.</p></p>
