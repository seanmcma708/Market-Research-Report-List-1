<p><p>Coumarin-based fluorescent whitening agents (FWAs) are organic compounds derived from coumarin that enhance the brightness and whiteness of various materials, particularly textiles, paper, and detergents. The demand for these agents is driven by the growth of the textile and consumer goods industries, where high-quality finishes are crucial for competitive differentiation.</p><p>The Coumarin-based Fluoroscent Whitening Agents Market is experiencing significant growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 10% during the forecast period.
