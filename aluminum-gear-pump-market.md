<p><p>The aluminum gear pump market is categorized into internal aluminum gear pumps and external aluminum gear pumps. Internal aluminum gear pumps feature gears rotating within the pump housing, providing efficient fluid transfer with low pulsation, ideal for high-viscosity fluids. External aluminum gear pumps, on the other hand, have gears mounted outside the housing, suitable for lower viscosity fluids and applications requiring higher flow rates. Additionally, the "others" category encompasses specialized designs and variations tailored for unique industrial needs.</p></p>
