<p><ul><li>Single Channel Counting System</li><li>Multichannel Counting System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photon counting systems are categorized into single-channel and multichannel counting systems. Single-channel counting systems focus on detecting light photons from one source, ensuring high sensitivity and speed, making them ideal for applications like single-photon detection. In contrast, multichannel counting systems can simultaneously capture and analyze photons from multiple sources, enhancing throughput and data acquisition across various applications. These systems are widely used in fields like biomedical research, quantum optics, and telecommunications, facilitating advanced detection capabilities and analysis efficiency.</p></p>
