<p><ul><li>Online Remote Sensing</li><li>Offline Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Onboard temperature sensing devices are categorized into two primary market types: online remote sensing and offline sensing. Online remote sensing involves real-time data collection and transmission, allowing for continuous monitoring and immediate analysis of temperature changes in various applications, such as industrial processes and environmental monitoring. In contrast, offline sensing involves data collection at specific intervals or events, often requiring manual retrieval and analysis, making it suitable for applications where continuous monitoring is less critical.</p></p>
