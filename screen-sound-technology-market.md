<p><strong>The Screen Sound Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cantilever Piezoelectric Ceramic Sound</li><li>Screen Exciter Sound</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Screen Sound Technology encompasses various innovations for enhancing display sound systems. Cantilever Piezoelectric Ceramic Sound utilizes piezoelectric materials that vibrate when electric currents are applied, generating sound directly from the screen's structure. This design allows for thin, lightweight speakers integrated into displays. In contrast, Screen Exciter Sound employs transducer technology to convert a screen's surface into a sound source, distributing sound through the panel. Both methods aim to create an immersive audio experience without bulky external speakers.</p></p>
