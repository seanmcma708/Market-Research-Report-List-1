<p><p>The ambient lighting software market encompasses several types of lighting solutions designed to enhance a space's atmosphere. Surface-mounted lights are fixed to surfaces for a seamless look, while strip lights provide versatile illumination along edges or contours. Track lights offer adjustable lighting, allowing users to focus on specific areas. Each type plays a unique role in shaping the overall lighting experience.</p></p>
