<p><p>The Microwave Wireless Monitoring System market is divided into two primary types: analog and digital microwave wireless video transmission. </p><p>Analog microwave wireless video transmission involves continuous signals that transmit video footage via radio waves, offering simplicity and lower costs, but with limited image quality and range. On the other hand, digital microwave wireless video transmission encodes video data into digital signals, providing higher image quality, better noise resistance, and enhanced security features, making it suitable for demanding surveillance applications. Both types cater to distinct monitoring needs.</p></p>
