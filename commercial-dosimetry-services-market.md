<p><strong>The Commercial Dosimetry Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OSL Technology</li><li>TLD Technology</li><li>CR-39 Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Dosimetry Services market comprises various technologies for measuring radiation exposure. OSL (Optically Stimulated Luminescence) technology offers high sensitivity and reusability, making it popular for personal dosimetry. TLD (Thermoluminescent Dosimetry) technology measures ionizing radiation by storing energy in crystals and releasing it as light upon heating. CR-39 technology utilizes a plastic track detector for neutron and charged particle measurements, providing accurate results in radiation environments. Each technology serves distinct applications in occupational health, environmental monitoring, and regulatory compliance.</p></p>
